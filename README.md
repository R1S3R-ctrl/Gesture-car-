# Gesture Controlled car using arduino
Taking the very first step in learning arduino and getting introduced to the amazing field of robotics by attempting to make a gesture controlled car with arduino
## Components:
### 1. Arduino Lilypad - 1
### 2. Motors - 4
### 3. L298N motor driver - 1
### 4. MPU6050 accelerometer - 1
### 5. Charging module - 1
### 6. Arduino Uno - 1 
### 7. Rechargeable batteries with their holder
### 8. nRF24L01
### 9. Jumper Wires
### 10. Breadboard
